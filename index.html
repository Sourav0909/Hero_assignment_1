Frequently Asked Questions

How do Team passes work?
Team passes cover up to 10 attendees and include exclusive perks like private workshop seating and a dedicated lounge.
When will the full schedule be released?
The complete agenda, including specific speaker times and workshop tracks, will be published two weeks before the event.
What is the difference between the Standard and Pro passes?
Both grant full 3-day access, but the Pro pass adds unlimited workshops, a VIP dinner, 1-on-1 mentorship, and dedicated lounge access.
Will the talks and keynote sessions be recorded?
Yes, all ticket holders get 30-day access to digital recordings of the main talks after the event concludes.
Can I get a refund if I can no longer attend?
We offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to a friend for free.
